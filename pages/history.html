CLUB HISTORY

Invictus Athletics Club was formed in 2016 by Head Coach Ray who has a wealth of coaching and knowledge and experience from his time coaching with multiple other clubs in the Waterloo Region.

The culture around the club that we strive to create is one of continuous growth. Everyday you show up to our practise is an opportunity to grow and leave a little better than you came in. After a few years in the Invictus program, the growth of each athlete's skillset and ability is evident.

As a club we have graduated better than 100 athletes since 2016 to various universties throughout North America, and for Coach Ray individually in his cocahing career, that number is far greater still. Invictus Athletics Club is a constant at Athletics Canada, Athletics Ontario, Royal Canadian Legion, and Minor Track Association competitions, always having a strong showing.

Our athletes have achieved great things in Canadian Track and Field, with numerous athletes getting the chance to represent Team Canada and Team Ontario at various competitions over the years.

We focus on elementary and highschool athletes who are dedicated to improvement and wish to compete in track and field at the next level, whatever that level may be for the athlete. We also welcome our senior atheltes back to the club for summer training sessions as the mentorship and experience they bring is invaluable.
